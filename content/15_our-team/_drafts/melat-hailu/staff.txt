Description:

As the Young Parents Assistant Director, Melat provides encouragement and support to young moms (ages 15-25) by leading the downtown Toronto support group, as well as volunteer mobilization and event-planning for the greater Young Parents ministry.

Melat is passionate about building community, fostering healthy relationships and accompanying young moms on a journey towards wholeness.
