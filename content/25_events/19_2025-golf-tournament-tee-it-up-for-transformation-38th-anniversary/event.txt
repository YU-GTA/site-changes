For **38 years** Youth Unlimited’s annual charity golf tournament has raised significant funds and built long-lasting partnerships that daily impact young lives throughout the GTA. 

Join us on 
**Tuesday, May 6, 2025
at King's Riding Golf Club" 
in King City, Ontario.

**DETAILS**
• Shotgun start at 10:00am
• Lunch & Dinner included
• 7th annual "**Pitch & Pledge**" will kick-start YU's large-scale fundraising campaign to reach almost 1,000 children and youth in our various summer programs across the GTA.
• We’ll have you out the door by 6:15pm!
• Spouses/partners are invited
to join us for dinner & our evening
program for an additional $50

• Single Golfer — $450
• Foursome — $1,800 
• Dinner Only — $50

**SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES**
• Hole Sponsor — $450
• Cart Sponsor — $3,000
• Meal Sponsor — $3,500
• Major Sponsor — $4,500 (+1 free golfer)
