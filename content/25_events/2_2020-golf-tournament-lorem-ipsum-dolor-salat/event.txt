**We are eager to see you for a safe and delightful 34th annual ‘Tee It Up For Transformation’ charity golf tournament in support of Youth Unlimited on Tuesday, June 15!**

 **Registration Details:**

- Cost: $400 for single golfer(s), $1600 for a foursome.
- Sponsorship: See the registration page for sponsorship options (100% of funds go directly to the ministry of YU). 
- We are doing a staggered start this year!

Foursome hosts will be able to choose a one-hour window for their tee time.
Single golfers will be accommodated as best as possible with their preferred tee time.

- To minimize contact at the tournament, you can also directly support the work of YU at registration by:

Purchasing your mulligans!
Entering the putting contest!
Making an early pledge for our annual Pitch & Pledge!

 

**Tournament Details:**

** Here are a few other important details for your safety and enjoyment:**

- We will follow all COVID protocols as directed by ClubLink for a safe and enjoyable experience for all.
- We will have a live digital leaderboard throughout the day!
- We will send you home with a meal gift card for you and your family to enjoy dinner together after the tournament!
- We will serve you a delicious meal on the course as arranged safely by ClubLink.
- We will spread the fun of our usual “Pitch & Pledge” throughout the golf course for you to have a blast giving to this year’s impact cause.
