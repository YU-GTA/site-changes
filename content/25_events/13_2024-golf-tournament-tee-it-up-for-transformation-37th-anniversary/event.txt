For **37 years** Youth Unlimited’s annual charity golf tournament has raised significant funds and built long-lasting partnerships that daily impact young lives throughout the GTA.

### Join us on
**Wednesday, May 15, 2024
at Wyndance Golf Club**
in Uxbridge, Ontario.

### **DETAILS**
• Shotgun start at 10:00am
• Lunch & Dinner included
• Our 6th annual “Pitch & Pledge” will kick-start YU’s large-scale fundraising campaign to reach almost 1,000 children and youth in our various summer programs across the GTA
• **NEW!** Spouses/partners are invited to join us for dinner & our evening program for an additional $50
• We’ll have you out the door by 6:15pm!

• Single Golfer — $450
• Foursome — $1,800
• Dinner only — $50

SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES
