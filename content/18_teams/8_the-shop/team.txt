Textone:

**Shop Job Placement**

Youth aged 15-25 are supported to find a job through resume building and job search support

**Shop Job Skills Mentoring** 

Youth aged 15-25 gain skills in basic automotive, small engines repair and other trades in our fully-equipped multi-trade mentoring and skills training center located in Newmarket

**Shop Paid Work Experience**

Youth aged 15-25 are provided with the opportunity to gain valuable work experience through our fully-equipped multi-trade mentoring and skills training center located in Newmarket
